<Info>
 **Quick answer:** Earn to Own (EtO) is WLTH's free gamified program. Complete missions, earn XP, climb the leaderboard, and top participants receive **tokenized pre-IPO Slice** allocations, not cash or WLTH token payouts.
</Info>

## Program basics

<AccordionGroup>
 <Accordion title="What is Earn to Own (EtO)?">
 WLTH's gamified rewards program. Complete missions, earn XP, and top leaderboard participants receive tokenized pre-IPO equity **Slices** in companies such as xAI, SpaceX, and others, without investing upfront.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Who can participate?">
 Anyone **18+** worldwide, subject to local laws. Check that participation is permitted in your jurisdiction.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="How long does each campaign run?">
 Most campaigns run about **two weeks**. Larger prize pools may run longer. Dates are shown in the app and on the campaign page.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Do I need to pay to participate?">
 **No.** Participation is free. Optional on-chain missions may involve small gas fees.
 </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Rewards

<AccordionGroup>
 <Accordion title="What are the rewards?">
 Tokenized pre-IPO equity **Slices** in the campaign company. No WLTH token, cash, or stablecoin payouts. Awarded Slices are immediately tradable on the WLTH Marketplace.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="How are rewards distributed?">
    After the campaign ends and XP is finalized, Slices are airdropped to winners. Distribution happens right after close. Check **Portfolio → Rewards** in the app.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Can I sell or transfer my Slice?">
 Yes. Slices are freely transferable and tradable from the moment you receive them.
 </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## XP and leaderboard

<AccordionGroup>
 <Accordion title="What are XP and the leaderboard?">
 **XP (Experience Points)** come from missions. Your rank determines your reward share. Example mission values (see app for current rules):
 - Referrals: +25 XP each, +250 XP bonus for 10 verified referrals
 - Social missions: +10 XP
 - On-chain missions: +15–20 XP
 - Daily streaks and bonuses as shown in-app
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="How is XP verified?">
 Automated and manual checks (social proof, wallet activity). Verification completes within **one week** of campaign close.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="What if I'm not on the leaderboard?">
 If you complete compulsory missions, you still receive an XP boost to kick-start the next Earn to Own campaign.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="How are ties handled?">
 Ties may be broken by who completed missions earlier or another fair method announced in advance.
 </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Eligibility and privacy

<AccordionGroup>
 <Accordion title="Are WLTH employees eligible?">
 **No.** Team members, contractors, and campaign administrators are excluded.
 </Accordion>
 <Accordion title="Is my data shared?">
 **No.** WLTH does not sell user data. Information stays within WLTH and is used only to verify participation and maintain campaign integrity.
 </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
