# The Alpha Fund

The **Alpha Fund** is a diversified portfolio of early-stage Web3 projects with a focus on **AI, Bitcoin Layer 2s, Real-World Assets (RWAs), DePIN (Decentralised Physical Infrastructure Networks), and Web3 Gaming**.

It gives investors early exposure to some of the most anticipated technologies and startups building the next generation of Web3.

***

A platform to create, register, and trade customizable Web3 identities. Communities can launch their own top-level domains (TLDs) and unite under a shared brand.
