# How to Export Your Private Key

1. Log in using the correct wallet associated with your account.
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
3. Select “Manage Profile” from the dropdown.
4. Click “Export Keys” and securely copy your private key.

👉 Once copied, you can import it into MetaMask or any other EVM-compatible wallet. This will give you access to all supported chains tied to that key.

🛡️ Important: Keep your private key safe and never share it with anyone. Treat it like your crypto master password.
