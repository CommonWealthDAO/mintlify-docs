## Information we collect

WLTH collects the following through the App:

- **Personal data**: name, email, public wallet address, phone number, government ID (for KYC/AML), and other information you provide. We only collect data necessary for the purposes below.
- **Device information**: device model, OS version, IP address, and unique device identifier.
- **Usage data**: features accessed, transactions made, and time spent in the App.

## Legal basis for processing

We process personal data on these grounds:

| Basis | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Contract** | Provide App access and features |
| **Legal obligation** | AML/KYC and regulatory compliance |
| **Legitimate interest** | Fraud prevention, improvements, support (when not overridden by your rights) |
| **Consent** | Marketing communications (where required) |

## How we use your information

- Provide and operate the App
- Process transactions and verify identity
- Prevent fraud and illegal activity
- Improve the App and develop features
- Send security updates and service announcements
- Send marketing (with consent where required)
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

## Data retention

We retain personal data as long as needed for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law.

## Your data protection rights (GDPR)

You may have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erasure of your data
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing

Contact **support@common-wealth.io** to exercise these rights.

## Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal data, including encryption in transit and at rest. No internet transmission or storage method is completely secure.

## Children's privacy

The App is not intended for users under **18**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal data.

## Changes

We may update this policy and will post the new version in the App.
