WLTH is reimagining what access to wealth looks like. For too long, the biggest opportunities (pre-IPO equity, early-stage funds, and automated strategies) were locked behind gatekeepers and high minimums. WLTH opens that access through fractional, tokenized investing starting from as little as **$20**.

<Info>
  **What is WLTH?** A tokenized investment platform on Base that gives everyday investors economic exposure to pre-IPO companies through tradable **Slices** (ERC-721 NFTs), curated funds, and gamified **Earn to Own** campaigns.
</Info>

## What you can do on WLTH

- **Invest** in pre-IPO companies and curated Web3 funds via Slices
- **Trade** Slices on the WLTH marketplace for secondary liquidity
- **Earn** tokenized equity allocations through Earn to Own missions
- **Participate** in the WLTH token economy, staking, and governance
